The Impossible Dream

As a former factory worker and service crew, I have experienced first-hand how working in the call center changed my life. And so we built our dream on sharing this experience with others. When we first thought of providing a free call center training for everyone, a lot of people said itâ€™s never going to work. People around us said that if we do this, we will not last long. Nobody believed in us, but we pursued anyway. And so we started - February of 2015, in a small barangay hall, as a local government project, we had our very first class. And it was a success! Almost everyone landed a job in the call center. And from there, was history. Our trainees would get hired every single week. Week after week. More and more trainees came in, and more and more got hired. (all of them were first timers). Eventually, we had to relocate because we couldn't fit in the barangay hall anymore. And from there we helped even more people land a job in the call center.

People from all walks of life come to us because we treat everyone with respect, and like a family. We exert 101% effort to help anyone who wants to learn, and we will not stop until there is a single person who needs us.

Even after 130 waves of trainees, and thousands of fresh call center agents later, we still train with the same passion and high level for FREE, and we always will.

If nobody believes in you, come to us. Because we will.

If you are interested, our training is always free and starts every Monday @ 11am in FRC Mall, Aguinaldo Highway Imus, Cavite. Come and join us now!
